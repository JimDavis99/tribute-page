Queen Elizabeth II

The Former Queen of England

About the queen:

  • Born in Mayfair, London in 1926
  • she married Philip Mountbatten, a former prince of Greece and Denmark
  • When her father died in February 1952, Elizabeth—then 25 years old—became queen of seven independent Commonwealth countries: the United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, Pakistan, and Ceylon (known today as Sri Lanka)
  • Elizabeth's only sibling, Princess Margaret, was born in 1930
  • In September 1939, Britain entered the Second World War. Lord Hailsham suggested that Princesses Elizabeth and Margaret should be evacuated to Canada to avoid the frequent aerial bombings of London by the Luftwaffe
